Privacy Policy for FoodNearMe

Effective date: May 29, 2025

1. Introduction

We respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from users. However, to provide the service of finding nearby food places, we request access to your device's location data.

3. Location Data

When you click "Find Food Near Me," the app will ask for permission to access your location. Your location is used solely to show nearby food spots on the map and is not stored or shared with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Geoapify and Leaflet to provide maps and location-based data. These services have their own privacy policies that you should review.

5. Cookies

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

6. Your Rights

You can choose not to share your location at any time. If you deny location access, the app will not be able to show nearby food places.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@foodnearme.food.
