About Us

Food Near Me is your go-to web tool for discovering delicious food spots around you in seconds. Whether you're craving a quick snack or a full-course meal, we make it easy to find what's nearby.

Our Story

We started this project with one simple goal: help people easily find places to eat wherever they are. Whether you're in your hometown or exploring new places, this app saves time and satisfies cravings.

Our Mission

To simplify the food-finding experience using accurate location data and up-to-date restaurant listings powered by Geoapify and Leaflet.
