Frequently Asked Questions

Can I find food near me without signing up?
Yes, no login or signup is needed.
Does the site show nearby restaurants on a map?
Yes, within 5 km radius.
Can I use it on my phone?
Yes, fully mobile-friendly.
Are all restaurant details free?
No, only basic info is free; more details require paid listing.
Is my location data safe?
Yes, we only use it to show nearby places, no storing or sharing.
Can I get directions to the restaurant?
Yes, routing from your location is available.
Does FoodNearMe.food deliver food?
No, we only help you find and navigate to food spots.
Can I suggest a restaurant to add?
Yes, use our feedback form to suggest or report.
